Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for a partnership?

All you have to do is head to http://connect.gokickback.co/apply/kickback and fill in the asked information - that’s it. It’s quick and easy.

How do I increase my revenue share?

You can submit a formal revenue share request to: support@gokickback.co we will evaluate your channel and inform you of the change.

How will I be paid?

We pay via PayPal at the start of every new month. Want to be paid via WU, Dwolla, or Paper Check? Submit a formal request to: support@gokickback.co

What are the requirements to join Kickback as a partner?

The basic requirements are 1,000 views per month but we do strongly check video content and you can still be accepted, if you have potential. If you have any copyrighted material and/or copyright-strikes on your channel, you will be declined instantly.

What is Kickback?

Kickback is a online media company that primarily focuses on creative content and audience growth. We are here to help content creators like yourself to reach a larger audience on YouTube and produce quality content. Kickback is a collaborative home for amazing personalities and original content.

Is there a required age to partner with Kickback?

We recommend everyone to be at least 13 years of age, however, people under the age of 18 have to get approval by their legal guardian(s).

How do I increase my revenue share?

You can submit a formal revenue share request to: support@gokickback.co we will evaluate your channel and inform you of the change.

How do I become part of Kickback staff?

Please see: https://kickback.recruiterbox.com

How do I become a network under Kickback?

You can compose and send a formal email at: networks@gokickback.co and someone from the network's management team will get back to you within 24 - 48 hours.
